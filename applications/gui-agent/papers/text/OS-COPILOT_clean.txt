Languageagentsrepresentacomplexsystemcomposedofalengthypipelinecomprisingvarious
components,eachrepresentingasubstantialbodyofresearch. Foracomprehensivediscussionon
themethodology,wedirectreaderstorecentsurveys(Wangetal.,2023b;Mialonetal.,2023a). In
