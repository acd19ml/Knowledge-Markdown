# CS6487 Final Group Project Description
# CS6487 期末小组项目说明

---

## Overview | 概述

| | English | 中文 |
|---|---------|------|
| **Project Type** | Group project based on a literature review | 基于文献综述的小组项目 |
| **Group Size** | Maximum 3 students per group; groups of 3 are strongly recommended | 每组最多 3 人，强烈建议 3 人组队 |
| **Sign-up** | Use the shared Google Sheet for group formation, topic selection, and the rubric | 通过共享 Google Sheet 完成组队、选题及查看评分标准 |

The final project of CS6487 is a group project centered on a **literature review** of a selected research topic. Each group will choose one topic from the list provided by the teaching team, read and organize relevant papers, and present a clear understanding of the area in both a written report and an oral presentation.

CS6487 期末项目为**文献综述**类小组项目。每组从教学团队提供的选题中选一，阅读并整理相关论文，通过书面报告和口头展示呈现对该领域的清晰理解。

**Goal | 目标：** Not simply to summarize papers one by one, but to **synthesize** the literature, **compare** representative approaches, **explain** their assumptions and trade-offs, and **identify** open questions or promising directions.

不是逐篇罗列论文，而是**综合**文献、**比较**代表性方法、**阐释**其假设与权衡，并**指出**开放问题或 promising 方向。

## 4. Deliverables | 交付物

### Written Report | 书面报告

| English | 中文 |
|---------|------|
| Present a structured literature review of the chosen topic. | 对所选题目进行结构化文献综述。 |
| Define the scope clearly, organize the literature coherently, compare representative methods, discuss strengths and limitations, and end with key takeaways or open directions. | 明确界定范围、有条理地组织文献、比较代表性方法、讨论优劣，并以要点或开放方向收尾。 |
| All sources must be cited properly using an appropriate academic citation style. | 所有引用须符合学术规范。 |

### Presentation | 口头展示

| English | 中文 |
|---------|------|
| In-class presentation on the chosen topic. | 课堂展示所选题目。 |
| Explain why the topic matters, how your group organizes the literature, what the main approaches or themes are, and what conclusions can be drawn. | 说明选题意义、文献组织方式、主要方法或主题，以及可得到的结论。 |
| Every group member must participate and be prepared to answer questions. | 每位成员均需参与展示并准备回答问题。 |

## 5. Evaluation | 评分

The detailed grading rubric is provided in the shared Google Sheet.  
详细评分标准见共享 Google Sheet 的 “Rubrics” 标签页。

| Component | Weight | 组成部分 | 权重 |
|-----------|--------|----------|------|
| Research Depth | 50% | 研究深度 | 50% |
| Presentation | 30% | 展示 | 30% |
| Report | 20% | 报告 | 20% |

> **Note on Research Depth | 关于研究深度：**  
> Research Depth is not a separate deliverable. It reflects the depth of your understanding and critical analysis of the literature, and will be assessed holistically based on both your written report and your presentation/Q&A (e.g., technical correctness, comparisons, and insights).
>
> 研究深度不是独立交付物，而是对文献理解与批判性分析深度的体现，将综合书面报告、展示及 Q&A 进行评定（如技术正确性、比较与洞见）。

**Quality expectations | 质量要求：**  
High-quality work should demonstrate depth of understanding, clear communication, and a well-organized review of the literature.

高质量作品应体现深入理解、清晰表达和有条理的文献综述。
